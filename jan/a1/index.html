Why I took Computer Studies

I've been taking graphic arts since 9th grade, and it ended up being my favouite class of every year. It's also one of the main reasons I decided to take computer studies, as I want to get more comfortable with the technology behind the programs that I use for designing. I've also been interested in how the more graphic side of websites are made, and I think that this course would make for a great intro.
