Fluffy Homemade Pancakes

Introduction

Pancakes are a classic breakfast favorite that is quick, easy, and delicious. I chose this recipe because it reminds me of weekend mornings with my family. This recipe is adapted from AllRecipes Pancake Recipe .

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Mix the flour, baking powder, and a pinch of salt in a bowl.
  2. Whisk the milk, egg, and melted butter together in a separate bowl.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients until smooth.
  4. Heat a pan to 350°F (about 175°C).
  5. Pour ¼ cup of batter onto the pan and cook until bubbles form. Then flip and cook until golden brown.
